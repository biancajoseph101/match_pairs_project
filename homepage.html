WHAT'S THE COLLECTIVE NAME?

PUT YOUR MEMORY SKILLS AND KNOWLEDGE OF ANIMALS TO THE TEST.

INSTRUCTIONS: MATCH EACH CARD CONTAINING A GROUP OF ANIMALS WITH THE CARD REPRESENTING THE COLLECTIVE NAME FOR THAT GROUP OF ANIMALS. THERE ARE FOUR ANIMAL CARDS AND FOUR RESPECTIVE "NAME" CARDS. TRY TO COMPLETE ALL THREE LEVELS.

EXAMPLE: WHAT'S THE COLLECTIVE NAME FOR A GROUP OF CAMELS? ANSWER: A CARAVAN.

*HINT: THE ANIMAL CARDS ARE REAL PICTURES WHILE THE "NAME" CARDS ARE CLIPART IMAGES.*
